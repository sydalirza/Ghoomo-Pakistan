About Us

Ghoomo Pakistan is a website directed towards presenting a positive image of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan in front of the world by showcasing the natural and historical beauties it possesses and how it is no more a threat to the world. As a great deal of interest was shown in the fields of tourism in Pakistan by the government since the war over terrorism was won by us, we hope to gather an international audience who have not been able to experience this heaven on Earth. From the deserts of Balochistan to the mesmerizing vallies of Kashmir, we aim to present a helpful guide for people who would like to visit Pakistan.
